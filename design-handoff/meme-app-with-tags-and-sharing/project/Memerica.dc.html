MEMERICA Freedom of the Feed

A tagged meme-sharing app for one shared space. The admin uploads memes (saved to their S3 bucket), assigns content tags, and decides when a post ships. Members just swipe a feed the admin curates with tags behind the scenes — then like, dislike, and comment.

Built on the ManaSync dark foundation with a red/white/blue skin. Blue = like, red = dislike. The flagship feed is live — swipe the feed, tap to react, open a comment thread. Tags are admin-only — members never see or manage them. iOS + Android shown for the core flows.
